The Dark Souls Gmail Extension is a Chrome extension that modifies Gmail’s “Message sent” notification with a Dark Souls–themed animation. We respect your privacy and want you to understand how this extension works with your data.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or sensitive user data. All functionality happens locally within your browser. No information leaves your device.

Permissions

The extension requests minimal permissions necessary to display the animation in Gmail:

  • activeTab – to detect when Gmail is active and apply the animation.
  • scripting – to inject the animation code into the Gmail interface.
  • Host permission (mail.google.com) – to allow the extension to run on Gmail only.

These permissions are used only for displaying the visual animation and do not grant access to your emails or personal data.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services.
